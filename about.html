Brief History

MEDOF MEDICAL CENTRE is a comprehensive medical outfit established purely with orthodox discipline with an avalanche of specialist units being undertaken by a myriad and cluster of professional specialists who are either qualified with their fellowship in Postgraduate medical/ Surgical disciplines or super consultants in one form of speciality or the other.

It was officially registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission in Kenya in November 2010.
Located along the popular Thika Super Highway, Witeithie. The location has been a geopgraphical advantage of being at the border-town of Thika and Nairobi. It is officially registered with the Ministry of Health.

Mission Statement

  • To provide holistic Healthcare delivery to the rural and semi-urban dwellers
  • To complement the ultra-modern medicare to urban and mega-city dwellers using the appropriate technolgy
  • To partner in public-private initiatives, employing the best of human resources to deliver accessible, affordable and acceptible quality service in line with the millenium expectation.
